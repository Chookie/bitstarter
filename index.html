Ever needed to just borrow a tool quickly and don't want the hassle of buying one? Or even something bigger like using a spare desk which someone may have in their office, just for a few hours whilst you are in town for the day? How about just borrowing someones time online to review a paper you are writing for school?

At Borrow Boy we make it easy to find someone in your area who can spare that tool for a day, or someone in the city who has a spare desk that they can let you have free or even just very cheaply, or someone on the other side of the globe who likes to share their experience as a writer.

By loaniing to you people gain recognition as loaners, making others more likely to loan to them and increasing the size of their borrowings. Think of yourself becoming a goodwill bank. We will keep people honest by allowing you to review and rate them as happens with many other services.

You can help make this happen by helping us, a small team of passionate, experienced software developers and professional borrowers (just ask my friends) to get this off the ground. We are already working on the project and hope to have the first version out soon but we would like to scale up our team and get more to market, even faster. We can borrow time by borrowing your investments.
